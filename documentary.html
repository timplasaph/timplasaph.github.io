Pampanga
Truly, Kapampangan, are famous for their very delicious cuisine most especially, their viands. There are a lot of famous restaurants and incorporated that sells a very delicious foods wherein the owner is a Kapampangan.
One of those restaurants is the Razon’s. They sell the famous and delicious halo-halo and their specialty which is the tocino. Pampanga’s best is a company that produce and sells tocino which Kapampangans are known of. Because of their skills in cooking, they made their own specialty which is known not only in the Central Luzon but also in whole country and that is Sisig. Sisig can be pork and chicken. Also, it has so many recipes and that patterns the original recipe of Sisig from Pampanga. With those skills that they have, Pampanga is known for the home of the famous cuisines.
According to a cook of “Bale: Lutong Kapampangan”, the secret why Kapampangan is known for cooking excellently is the process - a process in which they really put so much effort. For him, that process is on how they clean and prepare the ingredients. Most of all is the smile and love that they show every time they cook. He also stated that they are very particular with the measurement and the time.
Bulacan
Bulakenyos are known for their desserts like: pastillas, suman, and any other sweets that made use of rice. Since Bulacan, is one of the provinces in Central Luzon that produce rice not only for the country but also for export. The Bulakenyos’s creative minds have thought of making the kind of meal that usually for main course. Some of these desserts are termed as “kakanin” for its collective term.
According to Mrs. Emelita San Juan, a producer and maker of pastillas and many kakanin, the secret to achieve the perfectly done kakanin and pastillas is patience. Patience to wait for the consistency of different recipe to be perfect. As we all know, patience is really needed in making different kakanin because it will take time to have the desired texture and dense of the food. She also said that you should take note of the measurement and the texture. There are different ways to cook them.
It will always be sweeter when in Bulacan. The attitude of the Bulakenyos reflects in their different cuisine the prepare. Their cuisine will always be a reminder of how smart and creative Bulakenyos are.
